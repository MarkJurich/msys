What happened to the old FAQ?

The old FAQ is full of lots of good information. Eventually that information will be distributed to other places within www.mingw.org. In the mean time you can reference it from by clicking here.

Basically a decision was made to shorten the FAQ. Although the old FAQ provided a good source of information we felt that it was hard to maintain. We have therefore provided you with this document to replace it in the hopes of providing better service. If you feel that anything is missing please say so in the MinGW-users mail list.
